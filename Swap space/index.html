Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We're here to help

What is Swap Space?

Our mission is simple: Travel can be better and cheaper. Swap Space is a home exchange platform that allows members to swap their homes with others worldwide, enabling affordable and immersive travel experiences.

How does home swapping work on Swap space?

Members can list their homes on the platform and search for available properties in desired destinations. Exchanges can be mutual (you stay in each others homes) or non-mutual

How do I list my home on Swap Space?

After becoming a member, you can create a listing by providing details about your home, uploading photos, and setting your availability calendar. It's that simple to get started!

Are members verified?

We prioritise safety by verifying the identity and home address of our members. This includes checking official documents to confirm who they are and where they live. Verified members get a badge on their profile, making it easier to connect with trusted swappers.

Can I use Swap Space even if I don't own my home?

Yes! Since there are no cash exchanges between members, renters can also participate in home swaps. It's similar to letting a trusted friend stay in your home while you're away. However, we recommend checking your lease agreement or speaking with your landlord to ensure home exchanges are allowed.

How does pricing work? Is it free to Swap?

Listing your home on Swap Space is completely free! We only charge a simple annual or quarterly fee, which gives you unlimited swaps during that time. That means you pay once and get to travel as much as you want. No nightly fees, no hidden costs. Just seamless, stress-free travel with like-minded members around the world.
